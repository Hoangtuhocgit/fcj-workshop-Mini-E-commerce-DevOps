### Detailed comments

**Strengths**

- Completed **12 weeks of worklog** aligned with the AWS Study Group path, from fundamentals (EC2, IAM, VPC, S3) through migration, modernization, containers, and data analytics.
- Built a **development proposal** for Mini E-commerce DevOps Platform with target architecture, roadmap, budget, and risk assessment — demonstrating systems thinking and FinOps awareness.
- Delivered **hands-on workshop** deployment on local and AWS EKS with CI/CD and GitOps — proving ability to apply knowledge in practice.
- Attended **3 FCAJ events** (2 Community Days, 1 GAMEDAY), expanding AI, Cloud, and career-direction knowledge.
- Translated **6 official AWS blogs**, strengthening technical reading and Vietnamese technical writing skills.

**Limitations**

- Some weeks progressed slower than planned when facing infrastructure or configuration issues (EKS, GitOps, policy).
- **Presentation and communication** in group or mentor settings still need practice — sometimes explanations are lengthy or not concise enough.
- **Time discipline** (internal deadlines, AWS resource cleanup after labs) was not always consistent; improved through cleanup checklists and teardown habits.

### Needs improvement

- **Discipline and time management:** Clearer weekly planning and immediate AWS resource cleanup after each lab to avoid cost and deadline slips.
- **Problem-solving:** Prioritize logs, official documentation, and breaking problems down before seeking step-by-step help.
- **Professional communication:** Practice concise status updates, focused questions, and clearer workshop result presentations.
- **Certifications and technical depth:** Continue preparing for **AWS Cloud Practitioner** and **Solutions Architect** using FCAJ GAMEDAY and worklog topics.
